About This Tool

Have you ever wondered which accounts you follow on Instagram don't follow you back? Manually checking can be a tedious and time-consuming process, especially if you follow hundreds or thousands of people. This website was created to solve that exact problem.

Our tool provides a simple, fast, and secure way to compare your "followers" and "following" lists. By using the data files you can download directly from Instagram, our tool instantly generates a list of users who do not follow you back.

Your Privacy is Our Priority

We take your privacy very seriously. The entire comparison process happens directly in your web browser. This means your Instagram data files are never uploaded to our server or seen by anyone else. Everything is processed on your own computer, ensuring your information remains completely private and secure.

This project was created with the goal of providing a straightforward and useful utility for Instagram users. We are committed to maintaining a user-friendly and trustworthy service.
